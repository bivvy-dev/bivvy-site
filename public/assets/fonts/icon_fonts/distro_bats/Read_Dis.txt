DISTRO II BATS
Part of the DISTRO II family, a set of quality free fonts from Peter A


A dingbat font in the style of DISTRO II. Blurry, out-of-focus and fun. Use it as a DISTRO II expert set for arrows and bullets, or play with the smiley faces and fun symbols.

Check out the other fonts in the DISTRO II family:
DISTRO II LIGHT, MEDIUM, BOLD, HEAVY, BLACK
DISTRO II MIX
DISTRO II TOASTED
DISTRO II EXTINCT
DISTRO II LIQUORICE
and many more!
